Assigning Committees

Once you register, you will receive an email with the committee/assignment preference form. Most delegates will receive their assignments in early April; after that, assignments are released on a rolling basis depending on when you submit the form — first come, first served.

  • Preference Form: Sent via email after registration
  • Early Assignments: Released in early April
  • Remaining Assignments: Rolling basis (first come, first served)
  • Final Roster Due: April 24th — all delegate names + assignments
