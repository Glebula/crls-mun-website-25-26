Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending BridgeMUN.

Still have questions? Email: bridgemun25@gmail.com

Before You Arrive 4 questions
01

What is the dress code?

tap to reveal ↩

Business professional or Western business attire is required — suits, blazers, dress shirts, blouses, slacks, skirts, or dresses. Clothing must be appropriate for a school setting.

02

Are position papers required?

tap to reveal ↩

Requirements vary by committee — check your background guide. All work must follow BridgeMUN's Academic Integrity Policy.

03

What should I bring?

tap to reveal ↩
  • Notebook or legal pad
  • Writing utensils
  • Fully charged laptop (if permitted)
  • Printed research materials
  • Snacks (if allowed by school policy)
04

What if I need accommodations?

tap to reveal ↩

Inform your advisor and contact the Secretariat in advance so we can make appropriate arrangements for you.

Day of the Conference 7 questions
01

What time should delegates arrive?

tap to reveal ↩

Registration opens before the Opening Ceremony. Arrive on time and plan to stay for the full day unless prior arrangements have been made with your advisor and the Secretariat.

02

Is food provided?

tap to reveal ↩

Not provided — bring your own or head to Harvard Square (5 min walk): El Jefe's, Shake Shack, Pinocchio's, Felipe's, or Sweetgreen.

03

What behavior is expected in committee?

tap to reveal ↩
  • Engage respectfully in debate
  • Follow parliamentary procedure
  • Avoid personal attacks
  • Maintain professional decorum

Disruptive behavior may result in disciplinary action.

04

Can I use my phone during committee?

tap to reveal ↩

Limited and professional use only. Chairs may restrict phones during formal debate. All digital conduct must follow conference policies.

05

May I post on social media?

tap to reveal ↩

Yes! Use #BridgeMUN and tag @crls.mun. Get consent before posting photos of others.

06

What if I feel sick or there's an emergency?

tap to reveal ↩

Notify a Secretariat member, advisor, or CRLS staff immediately. Call 911 if urgent. Nearest facilities: CHA Cambridge Hospital & Atrius Health. See Medical Policy.

07

What awards are offered?

tap to reveal ↩
  • Best Delegate
  • Outstanding Delegate
  • Honorable Mention

Eligibility requires adherence to all conference policies.

Logistics & Contact 3 questions
01

Where is the conference held?

tap to reveal ↩

Cambridge Rindge & Latin School — 459 Broadway, Cambridge, MA 02138. Accessible by MBTA bus or Red Line at Harvard Square.

02

Where do buses and cars park?

tap to reveal ↩

Drop-off and parking details will be sent to registered advisors before the conference. Street parking and nearby public lots are available.

03

Who should advisors contact with questions?

tap to reveal ↩

Email the Secretariat at bridgemun25@gmail.com anytime before the event.
